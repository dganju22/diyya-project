Some Great Gluten Free Restaurants

1. Fresh Brothers

Fresh Brothers is a pizza chain with an AMAZING gluten free crust. For the health-concious, the califlower crust option is also gluten-free. Most of their locations have a dedicated gluten-free prep area to ensure no cross-contamination occurs. They have non-pizza gluten free options, such as salads and boneless baked chicken nuggets.





2. Mendocino Farms

Mendocino Farms is a trendy sandwich restaurant with locations throughout the US. Almost of all of their sandwiches can be made gluten-free with the subsitution of gluten-free bread. They also have delicious salads and deli sides/soups.





3. Active Culture

Active Culture is a health cafe with locations in Laguna Beach and San Clemente. Almost their entire menu of salads, bowls, drinks, and acai bowls is gluten free. The food is delicious AND healthy, and both locations are a short walk to the beach.
